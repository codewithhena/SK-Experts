ROC COMPLIANCE

Incorp. of Pvt. Ltd. Company

What is Included

  • All expenses and Govt fees (Up to Rs 1,00,000 Share Capital)
  • 2 Director Identification Number (DIN)
  • 2 Digital Signatures (DSC)
  • 1 Name Approval Application
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • PAN Number
  • TAN Number
  • GST registration
  • MSME registration
  • Drafting of Bank Account opening resolution
  • DSCs and DINs for extra Directors at extra cost

Documents Required

  • For a Company
  1. Four Proposed/desired Names of the company in the order of preferences
  2. Proportion of all directors in which they will bring the share capital
  3. Nature of business of proposed company (Main object)
  4. Office address proof of proposed registered office (Light Bill/rent agreement/consent letter/municipality tax bill)
  5. No objection Certificate (NOC) from the owner of the Property -Drafted by SK-tax)
  6. Mobile number and email id of the company (can be same as that of director)
  • For a Resident Director
  1. Copy of Pan Card, Aadhar Card and Passport size photo
  2. Mobile number, Email id
  3. ID Proof (Voter ID/Passport/Driving License)
  4. Address Proof (Bank Statement/Light Bill/Telephone Bill/Mobile Bill). The proof given must be in the name of director
  • For a Non-Resident Director
  1. Copy of passport
  2. All KYC documents Apostille with the Indian Embassy of that foreign country
  3. Mobile Number, Email ID and passport size photo
  • Common Points
  1. Educational qualifications of directors
  2. Place of birth of directors
  3. Since when living at present address (on given address proof)
  4. Address proof given should not be older than 2 months
